The "Loop of Excellence"

Our unique 4-step workflow ensures quality, relevance, and deep intellectual engagement at every stage.

1

Submission Portal

Student uploads draft/assessment. System recognizes discipline (STEM, Humanities, Law) and institutional requirements.

2

AI-Supervisor Collaboration

AI scans text for logical flow, citation consistency, and methodology strength. Flags areas of concern for the Professor.

3

Dynamic Lit Fetching

Identifies gaps and suggests 5-10 hyper-relevant, peer-reviewed journals and local South African sources.

4

Professor Review

Supervisor reviews suggestions on a unified dashboard. Adds personal notes to send "Augmented Feedback" to student.

Personalize Your Research Experience

We know AI can be intimidating. That's why Thuto Research Studio offers guided customization to fit your postgraduate needs, learning style, and language preferences.

Key Capabilities
