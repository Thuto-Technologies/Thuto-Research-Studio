The "Loop of Excellence"

A specialized workflow for the South African academic landscape. The AI works for the Professor, so the Professor can work for the Student.

1

Submission Portal

Student uploads draft. System recognizes discipline and institutional requirements.

2

AI Pre-Screen

AI scans for logical flow, citation consistency, and methodology strength. Flags areas of concern.

3

Literature Fetching

Identifies gaps and suggests 5-10 hyper-relevant, peer-reviewed journals and local South African sources.

4

Professor Review

Supervisor reviews suggestions on a unified dashboard. Adds personal notes to send "Augmented Feedback".
